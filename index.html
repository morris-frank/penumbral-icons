Theme-Safe Asset Generation

Icons that remain visible in every theme.

Custom emojis or app icons uploaded to work collaboration platforms like **Slack, Notion, or Linear** often suffer from a visibility problem. Dark icons become completely invisible in Dark mode, while light icons fade away in Light mode.

Penumbral Icons builds app-specific theme-safe emoji assets by either composing transparent icons onto an optimized solid-color background block matching the application's native dark interface (blends invisibly in Dark mode, stands out as a neat contrasting square in Light mode), or validating brand/canonical colors for sufficient contrast.

Deep Dive: Render Strategies

Composed composed

Theme-Blended Composition

Designed for monochromatic or single-color icons. The source SVG vector paths are tinted with the application's native dark foreground color (like light grey/white). The tinted icon is then overlaid onto a square bounding-box container filled with the application's exact dark background.

→ Blends invisibly in dark themes; floats as a beautiful square stamp in light themes.

Canonical canonical

Contrast-Validated Original

Designed for colorful logos or icons that must preserve their brand identity (e.g., Google Cloud, Gemini, Microsoft). Renders the original, multicolored SVG vector asset. The builder performs contrast checks to verify that the primary brand color maintains at least a 3:1 contrast ratio on both light and dark targets.

→ If contrast fails the threshold, the icon is automatically flagged for manual review.

Auto-SVG auto-svg

Dynamic SVG Swapping

The ultimate theme-aware standard. Generates dual light/dark CSS/HTML responsive SVG files. The icons are constructed using CSS classes or dynamic SVG attributes such as currentColor which automatically flip colors based on client-side styles or media queries.

→ Perfect for embedding in website frontends or responsive developer integrations.
